Project: Consumer Credit Default Prediction
-----------------------------------------
Dataset Metadata:
- Total Samples: 50,000 rows
- Total Features: 22 (demographics, credit history, income, loan amount, etc.)
- Target: Binary (1: Defaulted, 0: Paid back)

Current Model Configuration:
- Algorithm: XGBoost (Learning rate: 0.05, Max depth: 6)
- Validation: Stratified 5-Fold Cross-Validation

Known Data Issues:
- Class Imbalance: Approximately 20% of users defaulted (1:4 ratio).
- Missing Values: 5% missing data in 'Annual_Income' and 'Employment_Duration'.
- Feature Scale: Features like 'Annual_Income' and 'Age' have very different scales.
- Multicollinearity: 'Loan_Amount' and 'Monthly_Payment' are strongly correlated.
